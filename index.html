ASIAN AMERICAN STUDIES

This is a single scrollable webpage that serves as a visual-conceptual key, drawing from related sources, dialogues, and personal narratives of the Asian American diaspora. It requires user interactivity that takes place physically, pragmatically, and, with hopes, collaboratively.

For one to make speculations and theories on the cultural and societal phenomena within diasporic communities, one has to notice the patterns as well as the inconsistencies within those patterns.
